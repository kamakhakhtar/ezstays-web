Refund and Cancellation Policy

Once the Booking is Confirmed, we accept refunds on booking in following manner.

At Ezstays (A Unit of Next2Door Living Private Limited), we want to ensure your complete satisfaction with every booking. Please take a moment to review our Cancellation & Refund Policy to understand how we handle cancellations and refunds. By placing a booking on our website, you agree to abide by the terms of this policy. Cancellation Policy:

We do not offer cancellation on our booking. After 1 Hours, cancellations may still be possible, but a cancellation fee may apply. Please contact us immediately if you wish to cancel your booking.

Refund Policy:

Refunds will be issued in the following circumstances:

Cancelled Orders: If you cancel your booking within the specified time frame and meet the conditions outlined in our cancellation policy, you may be eligible for a refund.

Other Circumstances: Refunds may also be issued at our discretion in other exceptional circumstances.

Refund Process:

To request a refund, please contact our customer service team at +91 9000 699 900 with your booking details and reason for the refund request. Our team will review your request and guide you through the refund process, which typically takes 7 days to process.

Contact Information:

For inquiries about cancellations or refunds, please contact our customer service team at +91 9000 699 900 and email us at info@ezstays.in. Our team is available to assist you and ensure a seamless experience.

Thank you for reviewing our Cancellation & Refund Policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Your satisfaction is our priority at Ezstays.
